The Problem

The problem we are trying to solve is malnourishment.

  • Over 820 million people worldwide are chronically undernourished each year.
  • Every day, approximately 9 million children under five suffer from severe acute malnutrition.
  • Malnourishment contributes to nearly half of all child deaths globally.
